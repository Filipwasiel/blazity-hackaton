# Product

**One Idea, Every Format** — a locally-run web app for Blazity's "AI for
Content" hackathon. It removes the busywork of reformatting one content idea
for every channel.

## Problem

People who publish across channels rewrite the same idea by hand for each
platform — different length, tone, and structure every time. That reformatting
is repetitive and error-prone, and exactly the kind of work AI is good at.

## What it does

A single idea (a sentence or short paragraph) expands in one action into six
ready-to-use, platform-tailored outputs:

- **Tweet** — under 280 chars, with a hook
- **LinkedIn post** — professional, 3–5 short paragraphs, ends with a question
- **Newsletter intro** — warm, 100–150 words to open a send
- **Article outline** — H1 + 4–6 H2 sections with one-line descriptions
- **Short video script** — hook / body / CTA for Reels, Shorts, TikTok
- **Image prompt** — descriptive prompt for Midjourney or DALL-E

Supporting features: a tone selector (Professional / Casual / Bold) applied
across all outputs, inline editing of each block before copying, one-click
clean-text copy per format, and a localStorage history of the last 10 ideas.

## Target users

Solo creators, marketers, and founders who maintain a presence across several
channels and want to go from idea to copy-ready drafts fast.

## Scope and judging

Judged on thinking over volume — a small tool that does one thing well and can
be demoed and explained. Critical path: input → generate all six → copy.
Everything else is secondary. Deliberately no accounts, no backend, no database.
